Fonts

The site uses the CHANT font which contains non-standard characters, including OBI characters. Please download the CHANT font, double-click the downloaded file, and install the font in your operating system.

If there are characters which still do not render, please download this file, unzip it and install the two fonts (mingliu.ttc and mingliu.ttc) it contains.

Search help

  • Select whether you want to search
    • in Bingbian (BB) documents
    • in CHANT documents
    • in both Bingbian and CHANT documents
  • Select whether you want to search
    • in the (Chinese, OBI) text of the documents
    • in Ken-ichi Takashima'stranslation (only for BB documents)
    • in the titles of the documents（e.g. "BB-096", "H-00376-正")
  • Enter a search term. Traditional Chinese characters and OBI characters (in the CHANT font) can be used.
  • Click the "Search" button.

Display

In the hitlist, if you have more hits than are displayed, you can access the remaining hits by dragging down below the bottom of the hitlist.

Individual inscriptions are displayed in the hitlist. Click the document icon to the left to view all inscriptions occurring on the same the shell or bone.

The Heji image of the shell or bone is displayed in the upper right corner. You can zoom in on regions of the image by mousing over it.
